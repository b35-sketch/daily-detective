Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Daily Detective ("the app", "we", "us") is built by Cooba Dev. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data we collect

Daily Detective is designed to run locally on your device. We do not operate accounts, servers that store your gameplay, or analytics that identify you personally.

  • Gameplay data (cases played, verdicts, settings) is stored only on your device.
  • No personal information (name, email, location) is collected by the app.
  • No third-party advertising or tracking SDKs are included.

Apple App Store

If you download the app from the App Store, Apple may collect standard install/diagnostic data per their own privacy policy. We do not receive personally identifying information from Apple.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
